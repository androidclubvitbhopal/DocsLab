# Getting Started with Open Source

Welcome to the open-source initiative of the Android Club! This section is designed to help you make your first contribution.

## Why Contribute?

- **Learn by doing:** Work on real-world projects.
- **Build your resume:** Public contributions are visible to recruiters.
- **Community:** Join a network of passionate developers.

## Next Steps

Head over to our [GitHub Contribution Guide](/docs/open-source/github-contribution-guide) to learn the workflow.
